The best learning experience.

The Odin Project is one of those "What I wish I had when I was learning" resources. Not everyone has access to a computer science education or the funds to attend an intensive coding school and neither of those is right for everyone anyway. This project is designed to fill in the gap for people who are trying to hack it on their own but still want a high quality education.

Eligibility:

  • Open: Highlights that anyone, regardless of background, is welcome.
  • Self-driven: Emphasizes that motivation and initiative are key.
  • Inclusive: Underlines that there are no exclusionary criteria.
  • Accessible: Focuses on the project's free and online nature.
  • Curious: If you're truly curious coding, you're eligible.

Requirement:

  • Dedication: Learning to code demands time and focused effort.
  • Motivation: The Odin Project is self-guided,so you'll need internal drive.
  • Linux: While not strictly mandatory,a Linux-based OS (or understanding of how to use one) is highly recommended.
  • Curiosity: A willingness to explore, experiment, and tackle challenges is key.
  • Resilience: Coding comes with problem-solving and debugging. Persistence is essential.
